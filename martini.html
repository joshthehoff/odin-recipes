Martini Recipe (makes one serving)

Ingredients
  • 2 1/2 oz gin, preferably Plymouth
  • 3/4 oz Dolin Dry Vermouth
  • 1 dash Regan's Orange Bitters
  • lemon peel (for garnish
  1. Pour gin, dry vermouth, and orange bitters into mixing glass with ice.
  2. Stir ingredients together until as cold as Valley Forge.
  3. Strain drink into chilled coupe.
  4. Serve with garnish.
